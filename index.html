Short Bio

I am a Masters student in Computing Science at the University of Alberta, in Reinforcement Learning and Artificial Intelligence (RLAI) lab working with Prof. Richard S. Sutton.

My research is focused on representation for reinforcement learning. I'm particularly interested in forming agent's representation based on direct experience with the environment. I am also intereseted in using animal learning experiments as testbeds for evaluating performance of learning algorithms.
